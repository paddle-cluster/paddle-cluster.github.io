Mona Lundin

Mona Lundin is Associate Professor at the Department of Education, Communication and Learning at the University of Gothenburg. Her research concerns learning and knowledge in professional activities.

  • BalancED (Co-Investigator)
  • SOCDEX (PhD Supervisor)

Mona's dissertation project (funded by LearnIT in collaboration with the Knowledge Foundation), “New Web-based Educational Environments as Contexts for Interaction and Learning for Professionals,” the focus was on how professionals participate in and make use of Web-based educational platforms designed to facilitate various types of continuing education and professional development efforts. The main point was to analyse activities being developed in these new contexts for learning. What learning and discourse cultures are being established in these contexts?
