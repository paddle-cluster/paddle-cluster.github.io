PADDLE

Platforms, Automation, Digital Data, Learning & Expertise

PADDLE is a research cluster with members from the GothenburgGroup of learning and IT researchers at the University of Gothenburg. We work at three different departments, the Department of Applied IT, the Department of Education, Communication and Learning, and the Department of Pedagogical, Curricular and Professional Studies. While we work from a number of different research traditions and across different fields, our common interest is in understanding how networked technologies are changing the ways that we learn and develop expertise.
